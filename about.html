Hi!

I'm a UI / UX Designer and Front-End Developer with a passion for simple design and usability.

I am enthusiastic about UI / UX design and web development because they encompass everything I love: psychology, design, and technology.

As a naturally curious and empathetic person, I don't just want to create products that look pretty. I want to learn what motivates users to help create beautiful, user-centered products that are a joy to use.

How did I get here?

After graduating from college, I volunteered as a front-end developer and designer for a non-profit organization that helped survivors of abuse access anonymous group facilitated counseling. That's when I realized how much technology can change people's lives.

Since then, I have taught and mentored dozens of students on UX / UI and front-end web development. I can't get enough of this field because I love the challenge that every day brings.

Education

I earned my B.A. in Psychology from CSU Northridge to pursue my interest in empathy and human behavior.

Right now, I'm working full time and while earning a M.S. in Software Engineering at CSU Fullerton. One of my goals is to better understand how to build and design excellent software.

Hobbies

When I'm not working on websites, I enjoy hiking, running, and spending time with my two cats. I'm a creative at heart, and I enjoy crocheting, drawing, painting, and singing.
